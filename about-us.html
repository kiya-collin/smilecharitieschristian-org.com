ABOUT US

Smile Charities Christian Organization is south Sudaness national Organization legally registered with South Sudan Relief and Rehabilitation commission (SSRRC) Reg No: 4162. Smile Charities Christian Organization is non profits, non partisan, non governmental organization established in 2020 in Pagairinya Refugee camp Adjumani -Uganda , the organization was officially registered in the republic of south Sudan on March /24th /2022. The organization was formed to address socio economic,reduce poverty , climate change, social inequality and promotes Spiritual grow in south sudan society through charity and aid . we partner with the community in identifying their own problems and solving them using local initiative . SCOO AIMS is to assist women , youth , children and the disable to realized their full potentials by supporting and strengthening local institutions to enable community members response to peace and security beacuse all the area of development are limited when community and nations cannot lives in peace by ensuring that women , youth ,children and disable can develop in safe and cooperative Environment.
