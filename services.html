EXPERTISE IN PROTECTION

As stated above S.C.C.O have expertise in general protection, child protection, and GVB. We have expertise managing the entire department and we are active members of the cluster in the country both national and state includes the countries level. S.C.C.O project contributes to protection mainstreaming by ensuring that activities do not result in or perpetuate discrimination, abuse, violence, neglect or exploitation. The project implementations are informed by the core protection principles which include

Avoid exposing people to further harm as a result of the project activities

Ensuring people have access to impartial assistance and without discrimination

Protecting people from physical and psycological harm arising from violence and coercion

Assisting people to claim their rights,access available remedies and recover from the effect of abuse.Protection mainstreaming is achieved through priotizing safety and dignity and do not harm, ensuring meaningful access and accountability
